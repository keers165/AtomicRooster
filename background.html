ATOMIC ROOSTER

Tribute to Vincent Crane

30yrs after the death of Vincent Crane and 50yrs after the formation of Atomic Rooster, the Band have reformed. To celebrate the phenomenumnal sound that was Atomic Rooster the band will be engaging in a number of festivals in 2018
Originally planned to take in 10 venues in the UK, unforunenately Glastonbury is taking a fallow year.
