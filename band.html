ATOMIC ROOSTER BREAK THE ICE AT CAMBRIDGE ROCK

On August 3rd 1916 Atomic Rooster played the 100 Club, London, recieving great reviews from all the fans and papparazzi who attended. The following day the band appeared at the Cambridge Rock Festival, their first foray back in the festival scene.

French and Bolton have brought back to life an amazing back catalogue of music. Standout tracks tonight included 'Tomorrow Night', 'Black Snake', 'Save Me', 'Devil's Answer', 'Death Walks Behind You' and 'Breakthrough'.

With talk of further dates and a new album, Atomic Rooster could well be around for a while and judging by their gut bursting, fist pumping performance at Cambridge Rock, so they should be.
